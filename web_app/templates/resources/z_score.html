The interesting thing about the Z-Score is that it is a good analytic tool no matter what shape your company is in. Even if your company is very healthy, for example, if your Z-Score begins to fall sharply, warning bells should ring. Or, if your company is barely surviving, your can use the Z-Score to help evaluate the projected effects of your Enhancing efforts.

Everything has a value. Putting value in dollar terms is the cornerstone not only of running a business but also of investing in almost any form. Knowing how to arrive at a value for the physical and intrinsic characteristics of a business is essential to building wealth of all kinds. To that end, people who invest in companies need to look beyond the current state of the business they own (or want to own).
